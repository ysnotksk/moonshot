Track Overview

The AI & Machine Learning track brings together the brightest minds in artificial intelligence to explore cutting-edge developments, practical applications, and future trends. Whether you're a data scientist, ML engineer, or business leader, this track offers valuable insights into leveraging AI for competitive advantage.
Over three intensive days, you'll dive deep into transformer architectures, large language models, computer vision, reinforcement learning, and ethical AI deployment. Interactive workshops and hands-on labs complement keynote presentations from leaders at OpenAI, Google DeepMind, and leading startups.

What You'll Learn

  • State-of-the-art architectures: Transformers, GANs, and diffusion models
  • Production ML systems: MLOps, model deployment, and monitoring
  • Large Language Models: Fine-tuning, prompt engineering, and RAG systems
  • Computer Vision: Object detection, segmentation, and generative AI
  • Ethical AI: Bias mitigation, fairness, and responsible deployment
  • Business applications: ROI measurement and organizational adoption
