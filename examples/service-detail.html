Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical cloud migration take?
A standard migration takes 4-12 weeks depending on the complexity and number of workloads. Simple lift-and-shift migrations can be completed in as little as 2 weeks, while complex re-architecture projects may take 3-6 months.
Which cloud provider do you recommend?
It depends on your specific needs. AWS offers the broadest service catalog, Azure integrates well with Microsoft ecosystems, and GCP excels in data analytics and ML. We often recommend a multi-cloud approach for critical workloads.
Will there be downtime during migration?
No. We use blue-green deployments and traffic shifting to ensure zero downtime during migrations. All cutover operations are performed during low-traffic windows with instant rollback capability.
What's included in ongoing support?
Ongoing support includes 24/7 monitoring, incident response, monthly optimization reviews, security patching, and a dedicated account manager. We also provide quarterly business reviews with detailed reporting.
