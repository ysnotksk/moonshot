Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and process

How long does a typical project take?
Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A typical cloud infrastructure setup takes 4-8 weeks, while a full digital transformation project may take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during the discovery phase.
Can I switch plans after signing up?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. Our team will help ensure a smooth transition between service tiers.
What industries do you specialize in?
We have deep expertise in FinTech, Healthcare, E-commerce, and Education. However, our solutions are adaptable to any industry. We've successfully delivered projects across manufacturing, logistics, and professional services as well.
Do you offer a free consultation?
Absolutely. We offer a complimentary 30-minute discovery call to understand your needs and provide initial recommendations. Book a demo through our website or contact our sales team directly.
What support is included with each plan?
Starter plans include email support with 48-hour response time. Professional plans get priority support with 24-hour response and a dedicated account manager. Enterprise clients receive 24/7 dedicated support with a full success team.
