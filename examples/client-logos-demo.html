Usage Instructions

The Client Logos component is a perfect alternative to the stats section. Simply replace the stats section HTML with the client logos markup.

Features:

  • Smooth infinite horizontal scroll animation
  • Pauses on hover for better UX
  • Grayscale filter with color on hover
  • Fade edges for seamless appearance
  • Fully responsive (faster scroll on mobile)
  • Two variants: Stats style (with background) or Minimal (no background)

To Use:

  1. Replace your stats section with the client logos HTML
  2. Add your client logo images (SVG or PNG recommended)
  3. Duplicate the logo items for seamless looping
  4. Choose betweenms-client-logos--stats-style or minimal style
